# FinSolve Employee Handbook (General)

## Leave policy

Full-time employees accrue **18** annual leave days per calendar year. Carry-forward is limited to **5** days into the next year unless approved by HR.

## Remote work

Eligible roles may work remotely up to **2** days per week with manager approval. Client-facing roles may have stricter site presence requirements documented by the line manager.

## Code of conduct

FinSolve expects professional behavior, confidentiality of client data, and compliance with all applicable regulations. Report concerns through the ethics hotline listed on the intranet.

## Benefits overview

Medical insurance, parental leave of **12** weeks primary caregiver, and learning stipend of **50,000 INR** per year for approved courses.
