# FinSolve FY2024 Financial Summary (Internal)

## Revenue

Consolidated revenue for **FY2024** was **420 crore INR**, representing **14%** year-over-year growth compared to FY2023.

## EBITDA

Adjusted **EBITDA** for FY2024 was **96 crore INR** with a margin of **22.9%**.

## Budget allocation FY2025

Planned allocation: **Engineering 38%**, **Sales & Marketing 24%**, **Operations 18%**, **G&A 12%**, **Risk & Compliance 8%**.

## Investor relations

The board approved a dividend policy targeting **15–25%** of net profit for distribution, subject to regulatory capital requirements.

## Risk factors

Key risks include regulatory changes in digital lending, concentration in enterprise banking clients, and foreign exchange exposure on offshore contracts.
