# FinSolve Marketing — Q3 Campaign Performance

## Digital campaigns

Paid search and LinkedIn campaigns delivered **3.2M** impressions with a **2.1%** CTR in Q3. Cost per qualified lead improved **11%** versus Q2.

## Brand guidelines

Primary voice: **confident, precise, and compliance-aware**. Avoid superlatives that cannot be substantiated. Mandatory disclaimer blocks for regulated product pages are stored in the CMS snippet library.

## Competitor landscape

Primary competitors tracked: **ApexPay**, **LedgerOne**, and **NorthBridge FinCloud**. Differentiation focuses on RBAC-rich knowledge tools and audit-ready APIs.

## Market research

Survey of **200** CFOs in APAC indicated **67%** prioritize embedded compliance tooling when selecting fintech vendors.

## Co-marketing

Partner co-marketing assets require legal review and brand approval before external publication.
